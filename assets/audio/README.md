# Audio Assets

Place your audio files here:

- `bg_music.mp3` - Background music for the app (romantic instrumental)
- Add any voice notes or audio messages

## Recommended Audio:
- Format: MP3 or AAC
- Bitrate: 128-192 kbps
- Duration: 2-5 minutes for background music
- Volume: Normalized to avoid clipping

## Free Music Resources:
- YouTube Audio Library
- Free Music Archive
- Incompetech
- Bensound
